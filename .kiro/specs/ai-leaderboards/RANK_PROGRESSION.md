## Balancing Philosophy

### Why Sub-Ranks Work

1. **Frequent Rewards**: Rank up every 10 wins instead of 50+
2. **Clear Progress**: "Warrior II" shows you're 2/3 through Warrior tier
3. **Reduced Grind**: 10-win chunks feel achievable
4. **Tekken-Proven**: This system has worked for decades
5. **Long-Term Goal**: RPS-God X provides years of progression

### Preventing Burnout

- **Early game**: Fast progression (5-10 wins per rank)
- **Mid game**: Steady progression (10 wins per rank)
- **Late game**: Slower but prestigious (20-50 wins per rank)
- **End game**: Ultimate challenge (30-50 wins per rank)

### Maintaining Prestige

- Only 1% of players reach RPS-God tier
- RPS-God X requires 690+ wins (months/years of play)
- High ranks are visually distinct (cosmic gradients)
- Rank names convey power and achievement
